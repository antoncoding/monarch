# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Monarch is for advanced DeFi users, Morpho power users, fund managers, and operators who prefer direct control over their lending stack. Users come to the product to inspect markets, understand risk, manage positions, deploy vaults, and learn enough about the underlying system to make their own decisions.

## Product Purpose

Monarch provides a neutral, transparent interface for Morpho Blue lending markets and related vault workflows. It helps users compare markets, review oracle and liquidity risk, manage open positions, rebalance capital, deploy AutoVaults, and claim rewards without adding platform fees or hiding protocol mechanics.

Success means an informed user can see what is happening, understand the tradeoffs, and act deliberately. The product should not push deposits, manufacture urgency, or simplify away risk in order to drive conversion.

## Brand Personality

Neutral, precise, intelligent. Monarch should feel clean and useful to people who already take capital allocation seriously, with enough structure to guide exploration but no retail-style handholding or hype.

## Anti-references

Monarch should not feel like a simple retail dashboard, a hype-heavy crypto funnel, a toy-like yield app, or a persuasive UX-maxi product that nudges users into action before they are ready. Avoid deposit-pressure patterns, exaggerated emotional copy, gamified yield chasing, and over-simplified risk presentation.

## Design Principles

- Put control before conversion: workflows should expose choices clearly rather than pushing a preferred action.
- Show risk without theater: present oracle, liquidity, collateral, and rate context in plain terms with useful detail.
- Preserve expert density: make tables, filters, settings, and charts scannable for repeated analytical use.
- Be neutral about action: the interface should support deposits, borrowing, vault deployment, and rebalancing without encouraging users to do more than they understand.
- Teach through structure: help users learn the lending stack by revealing mechanics, provenance, and tradeoffs in context.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use sensible WCAG AA defaults. Respect reduced-motion preferences, avoid relying on color alone for risk or status, and keep chart palettes readable for users with common color-vision differences.
